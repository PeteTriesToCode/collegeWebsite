BIT: Bachelors of Information Technology


Duration: 3 Years Academic

NQF: Level 7

Credits: 360

SAQA ID: 94121

A bachelor's degree is often the minimum requirement for many professional roles, especially in IT. Investing in this degree can yield significant long-term benefits, including access to prestigious companies in the field and the knowledge and skills needed to start your own tech venture. In summary, pursuing an IT degree is a valuable investment in your future career prospects

This IT degree emphasizes information systems and offers a solid grounding in software engineering and business intelligence. You will explore a broad array of subjects, from mobile and wireless networks to artificial intelligence and intelligent systems.
Focusing on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, this program aims to develop you into a well-rounded professional, opening doors to various exciting career paths, such as mobile and web app development and game creation.
