Our Lecturers


      Mr Bob Sponge

      Meet Lecturer Bob Sponge, the most enthusiastic instructor you'll ever encounter! With a boundless passion for teaching, Bob's classes are always energetic and engaging. His infectious excitement and love for education make every lesson a memorable adventure, inspiring students to dive deep into their studies with enthusiasm and joy. His laugh might make your ears bleed.

      Classes: Web Programming, Linear Programming, Programming


      Dr Star

      Introducing Dr. Star Patrick, a laidback and easygoing lecturer known for his relaxed teaching style. However, don't let his calm demeanor fool you — Dr. Star has a surprising tendency to get really angry when provoked. Despite this, his unique approach to education and his ability to connect with students make his classes both unpredictable and memorable. Just DON'T make him angry.

      Classes: Math, English, Database Development, Cloud Native Applications


      Mr Fish Edwardson

      Introducing Fish Edwardson, an exceptionally strict lecturer known for his no-nonsense approach to teaching. Joking around is not an option in his classes, as he maintains a serious and disciplined atmosphere at all times. With a focus on rigor and precision, Fish Edwardson ensures that his students are always on their toes and fully engaged in their studies.
      We bet you 1 year's funding you can't make him laugh...

      Classes: Business Fundamentals, Ethics, English, Linear Programming, Alpha Math


      Mr Buzz Kill

      Introducing Mr. Buzz Kill, a renowned professor with a mysterious past. Rumored to have been an assassin before fleeing his homeland, Mr. Buzz Kill now dedicates himself to academia. He is known for his complete lack of emotion—neither happiness nor anger ever cross his face. His stoic demeanor and enigmatic background captivate students, making his classes both intriguing and profoundly educational.
      Rumor has it that students that fail his class, never see the light of day ever again.

      Classes: Web Programming, Database Development, Network Fundamentals, Cloud Native Applications, Computer Architecture
