Higher Certificate


Duration: 1 Year Academic

NQF: Level 6

Credits: 120

SAQA ID: 71850

The Higher Certificate in IT is designed to align with our mission of equipping young individuals with the skills to understand, utilize, and apply ICT in an effective, efficient, and ethical manner, ultimately leading to meaningful employment opportunities.

The rapid growth of data worldwide is astonishing. Big data has revolutionized how businesses operate and gain insights into their customers, enabling them to analyze vast amounts of information to detect trends, patterns, and predict future consumer behaviors.
Consequently, the need for skilled data professionals with advanced database expertise is on the rise. This qualification provides you with these essential skills, facilitating a swift entry into the job market. The expertise you acquire will empower you to address operational business challenges within the data science domain, ensuring your success in today's business landscape.
