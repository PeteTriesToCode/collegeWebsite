BCOM: Bachelors of Computer Sciences


Duration: 3 Years Academic, 1 Year Internship

NQF: Level 8

Credits: 506

SAQA ID: 62689

Our Bachelor of Computing degree equips you with fundamental skills in troubleshooting and developing effective solutions. You will also gain the ability to apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings across various fields, demonstrating best practices and innovative solutions.

You will acquire strong theoretical knowledge that is directly applicable to real-world scenarios. Additionally, the practical assignments and projects you engage in will mirror industry practices, helping you develop both business-specific and soft skills. These include effective communication, customer satisfaction strategies, teamwork, and the ability to mentor others.
In your fourth year of the Bachelor of Computing program, you will have the exceptional chance to undertake an internship with a national or international organization. You will also conduct academic research and present your findings in a formal dissertation.
