This dynamic diploma includes contemporary specializations tailored to meet current industry needs. These specializations will open doors to numerous exciting career paths and ensure you graduate with strong market demand.

Our Diploma in Information Technology is divided into two distinct phases: a foundational phase and a specialization phase. The foundational phase provides a broad understanding of the field, while the specialization phase is designed to be career-oriented.
This program aims to develop you into a well-rounded professional by emphasizing knowledge, practical execution, and both professional and hands-on skills. Additionally, you will have the valuable opportunity to engage in six months of in-service training either with an external company or in a simulated work setting.
